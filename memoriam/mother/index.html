In memory of Sau Wai Stanley

6th January 1945 - 18th June 2003

Sau Wai Stanley (née 林秀惠), 58, died in the early hours of June 18th, 2003. She parted us peacefully in her sleep after a seven month, strong and dignified fight with terminal cancer.

A mother, wife and sister, her vivacious spirit and will shone through every aspect of her life making the best of the difficult times, and celebrating the good times. To the great many other people that knew her, she was a loving and loyal friend.
